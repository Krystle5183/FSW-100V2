About Us

We serve many industries including the refining, petrochemical, oil and gas, utilities, power, OEM, and military. J.K. Kalb was established in 1934 by Joseph Karl Kalb as a manufacturer’s stocking representative Today, we continue to provide a wide selection of locally warehoused inventory from top quality manufacturers. We also supply customized products to meet unique application needs. In addition, we provide repair, calibration, and certification services, diaphragm seal assembly, switch assembly, rental of test and monitoring equipment, and procurement services. We are dedicated to providing our customers with products and services of the highest quality at a competitive price. Our knowledgeable staff has a combined experience of over seventy five years with an average tenure of seventeen years, and it is ready to assist you with your instrumentation requirements.
